Park City, UT (My hometown!)

Park City is an old silver mining town turned ski & mountain bike destination.

Things to do:
  • Ski the best snow on EARTH
  • Mountain Bike
  • Hike

Big Island, HI

The eco-diversity on the Big Island is one of the most striking features of the big island.

Things to do:
  • Snorkel/Dive
  • Fishing
  • Relax at the beach!

Newport, OR

Newport, Oregon is one of my family's favorite places to visit on the Oregon coast!

Things to do:
  • Surf
  • Visit Yaquina Head lighthouse
  • Eat at the Chowder Bowl!
